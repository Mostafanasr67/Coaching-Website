About

How It Started

I used to be a swimmer since I was 4 years, but as soon as I became 16 years old, I decided to change my physique and quit swimming. Starting a new sport wasn't an easy decision but being thin also wasn't going well. From 2019 till 2024, I have learned alot of skills and gained over 15kg of pure muscles. Moreover, Mastering the basics was my main goal at the beginning and achieving skills like the Muscle Up and Handstand were my primary goals and they didn't take a long time. On the other hand, Handstand Push Ups, Front Lever and Human Flag took 2 years to be fully unlocked.

How It's Going

Starting 2021, I started taking Calisthencis professionally targeting Competing in National and Regional Competitions. In addition, in 2022 I entered my very first competition seizing my first gold medal in a private competition ending 2022 with 2 gold medals. Moreover, I had only 6 months to get ready for my first National Competition and my only option to get qualified to WSWCF World Championship in Latvia, gratefully I seized the silver medal but unluckly only the first place get qualified. Then, I joind alot of competitons gainig alot of experience and medals. Finally, I am training for the next Nationals and I think it's ours.

Coaching Journey

My Coaching journey started in 2022 in Raw Egypt. I leaded 50+ passionates who didn't only change physcially but also mentally and their whole lifestyle proudly improved. After 2 years of Group Coaching, I started Online Coaching, changing beings' lifestyle remotely. Planning forward to develop the first Calisthenics app in the Middle East. Stay Tuned!
